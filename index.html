About Us

FRUITZA Poultry Farm & Hatcheries is a leading provider of high-quality **Kienyeji Improved Chicks**. We offer **healthy, productive chicks** at different growth stages to meet farmers' needs.

Our Mission

To provide quality, healthy, and productive chicks, empowering farmers with sustainable poultry farming solutions.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted hatchery for improved Kienyeji chicks, supporting poultry farming success across the region.
